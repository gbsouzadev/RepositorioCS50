This scratch project was developed to fulfill the problem set 0 of Harvard's CS50 course.

More info about the course at https://www.edx.org/cs50

Play this project/game on https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/723154278/

Instructions:
It is a baseball game. 
Use the arrow keys up and down to move the batter and hit the ball. 
You can also use your mouse to move the glove and catch the hitted balls.
It can be played by 1 or even by 2 individuals in the same PC or Notebook.
